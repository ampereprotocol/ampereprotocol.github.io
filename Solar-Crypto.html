How Ampere Protocol Works

Our innovative platform bridges the gap between renewable energy production and blockchain technology.

Solar Energy Generation

Connect your solar panels to our network and start generating clean, renewable energy while earning $AMPERE tokens.

Blockchain Verification

Our decentralized ledger records all energy production transparently and immutably on the Solana blockchain.

Token Rewards

Earn $AMPERE tokens based on your energy contribution, which can be traded, staked, or used within our ecosystem.

Ampere Protocol creates a peer-to-peer energy network where users can buy and sell excess solar energy directly to others in their community, all facilitated by smart contracts.
