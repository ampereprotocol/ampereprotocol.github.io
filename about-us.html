Our Story

Our Story

From vision to reality - our journey in renewable energy innovation

Founded in 2025, Ampere Protocol began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a decentralized platform that makes solar energy accessible and profitable for everyone. Our team of blockchain experts, renewable energy engineers, and financial specialists came together to build a solution that bridges the gap between clean energy production and cryptocurrency rewards.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the solar blockchain revolution, with thousands of users across multiple countries participating in our ecosystem.
